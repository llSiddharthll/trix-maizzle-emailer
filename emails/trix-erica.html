ERICA – Recessed Adjustable Downlight
Flexible illumination crafted for design-driven spaces.

ERICA is a deep recessed tiltable die-cast aluminium fixture created for projects that demand precision, adaptability and visual elegance.

With adjustable beam direction and multiple reflector finish options, ERICA allows lighting designers to shape spaces with greater intent while maintaining exceptional glare control.

Perfect for residential, hospitality, museums, retail and commercial applications.
