Compact Recessed Adjustable Downlight

MINIS

Small in Size.
Precise in Performance.

MINIS is a recessed adjustable downlight crafted to deliver precision illumination within a compact architectural footprint.

Built with die-cast aluminium construction and deep recessed optics, MINIS offers exceptional beam control, reduced glare and design flexibility for premium interiors.

With adjustable beam direction and multiple beam angle options, it is designed to highlight spaces with accuracy while maintaining visual comfort.

Perfect for residential, retail, hospitality and architectural applications.
